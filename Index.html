Who We Are

Advancing the Gospel
Across the United Kingdom

Third Watch UK is a Christ-centred evangelical movement committed to advancing the Gospel of Jesus Christ across the United Kingdom. We exist to raise disciples, strengthen believers, and impact communities through the Word, Evangelism, and Intercessory Prayer — driven by a passion to see lives transformed and communities revived through spiritual empowerment and practical acts of compassion.

Our Vision

Nations Awakened
unto Righteousness

To see nations, cities, families and individuals liberated and awakened unto righteousness and walking in it — therefore quickening them to become spiritually responsible to occupy their places until the return of our Lord.

Our Mission

The Gospel to
Every City and Nation

To evangelise and impart the life of God into every city, town, nation and people with the Gospel of Jesus Christ — which has inherent power to transform any life, city, or nation into the same image and likeness of God as revealed by Jesus Christ our Lord.
